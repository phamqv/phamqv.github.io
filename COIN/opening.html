Openings

Prospective Students: Please email Dr. Pham your resume, motivation letter, scholastic transcripts, and publications (if available). Although I may not respond individually due to the volume of applications, I will thoroughly read the emails and supporting documents as I look for new students. You will be contacted should further information is needed. Students working with us are usually highly self-motivated and with strong mathematical and problem-solving skills. You can get a sense of the style of our work by looking at our publications.

Prospective Researchers: Please check openings and contact us before your application preparation and application. Do give us a shout if you have any questions.
